About Me

I'm a final year Ph.D. student under the supervision of Prof. Dr. Anette Frank at the Department of Computational Linguistics at Heidelberg University and also a part of Research Training Group AIPHES.

My primary research interest areas are : Natural Language Understanding, Commonsense Reasoning, Sentiment Analysis, Knowledge-Based Reasoning, Explainable Machine Learning, Deep Learning for NLP.

I am interested in building computational models that can learn and understand natural language semantics, pragmatics and perform reasoning. I have completed my Master's under the supervision of Prof. Dr. Ditrech Klakow at Saarland University, Saarbr√ºcken, Germany.
