About Me

I am a Doctoral Student under the supervision of Prof. Dr. Anette Frank at the Department of Computational Linguistics at Heidelberg University and also a part of Research Training Group AIPHES. I have worked in Amplexor International as a Data Scientist for 3 months (January-March 2018). I have completed my Master's under the supervision of Prof. Dr. Ditrech Klakow at Saarland University, Saarbrücken, Germany.

My primary research areas are: Machine Learning, Natural Language Processing, Deep Learning, Information Retrieval, Data Mining and Convex Optimization.

Prior to this, I have completed Bachelor in Technology (B.Tech) in Computer Science and Engineering from Guru Nanak Institute of Technology , Kolkata.
